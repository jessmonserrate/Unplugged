Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the Unplugged retreat?

It is best to reach us by car. If you choose to drive yourself, you can locate directions here. If you would like us to come and pick you up, we are happy to make those arrangements with you. Please give us a call so we can understand how many people we will be transporting and when.

What kind of team is right for teh Unplugged retreat?

Your team is! We've worked with teams of all types and sizes and our expert staff is capable of accomodating any type of group or size.

What size teams can you accomodate?

We can easily accomodate groups of 100-200, however are able to accomodate larger groups as needed. We are also able to accomodate small groups (and delighted to as well).

Is there really no wifi available?

That's right- in fact, that's where our name comes from (see: Unplugged). In order to provide you with the best opportunity for team building, one of the key ingredients is the complete attention and focus of the team- that means that we don't want you to have any distractions while you're here.
